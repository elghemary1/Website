Hey, I'm Farah!

Educator • STEAM Advocate • Impact Creator

I'm a passionate educator, driven by curiosity to learn everything. I work with little engineers to build things that move and think.

As an educator and STEAM advocate, I believe in the power of hands on learning and problem solving. Over the years, I've developed programs that combine technology, art, and critical thinking to help students discover their potential.

When I'm not in the classroom, you might find me:

  • Volunteering with youth programs and community events
  • Running hands-on robotics and tech workshops
  • Sketching random ideas in my notebook
  • Taking photos of everyday moments
  • Getting lost on a hiking trail or planning the next one
  • Dancing (badly but happily)

Teaching Lab

My teaching philosophy centers on creating an environment where curiosity is encouraged, mistakes are celebrated as learning opportunities, and every student feels empowered to explore and create.

I believe in hands-on learning experiences that connect theoretical knowledge with real-world applications. Through project-based learning, students develop critical thinking skills, creativity, and confidence in their abilities.

  • Foster curiosity and a love for learning
  • Encourage creative problem-solving
  • Build confidence through hands-on experiences
  • Create inclusive learning environments
